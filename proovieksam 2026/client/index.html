Team Falcon represents a significant competitive legacy and culture that tells the story of our journey through esports and competitive gaming.

 

Founded in 2004, the team has been consistently restored and refined thanks to the dedication of players, staff, and fans.

Falcon was vital to the early competitive scene, securing key wins and becoming a central meeting point for talented players.

 

Today, Team Falcon is a popular brand and competitive powerhouse, offering a chance to see gaming history in action and

enjoy top-tier gameplay, standing as a living testament to the richness of our legacy.
